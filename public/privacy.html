Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

MCP Skills ("we", "us") is operated by Rise Above Partners LLC. This policy explains what data we collect when you use mcpskills.io and how we handle it.

What we collect

When you submit a GitHub repository URL for scanning, we collect:

  • The repository URL you entered (e.g., owner/repo)
  • The scan results generated by our algorithm
  • Basic request metadata (IP address, browser type, timestamp)

We do not collect your name, email, or any personal information through the scanner. If you contact us via email, we retain that correspondence.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not access your GitHub account or require authentication
  • We do not read your private repositories
  • We do not store GitHub API tokens from users
  • We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising

How we use your data

  • To generate and display trust scores for the repos you scan
  • To improve the accuracy of our scoring algorithm
  • To monitor service health and prevent abuse

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

MCP Skills uses the following third-party services:

  • GitHub API — to fetch public repository data for scoring. Subject to GitHub's privacy policy.
  • OpenSSF Scorecard API — to retrieve security posture data for public repos.
  • LemonSqueezy — to process payments for paid reports. Payment data is handled entirely by LemonSqueezy per their privacy policy. We do not see or store your payment details.
  • Netlify — to host the site and run serverless functions. Subject to Netlify's privacy policy.

Data retention

Scan results may be cached for up to 24 hours to improve performance. Request logs (IP, timestamp) are retained for up to 30 days for abuse prevention and then deleted.

Your rights

You can request deletion of any data associated with your scans by emailing hello@mcpskills.io. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be noted with an updated date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hello@mcpskills.io.
