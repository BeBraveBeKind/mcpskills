Certified Safe Badge Program

Prove your AI skill or MCP server meets the highest trust standards. Certified repos get a gold badge and fast-path trust for agents.

Requirements

– Composite score >= 7.0 / 10
– Solid (security) dimension >= 5.0 / 10
– No disqualifiers (archived, critical CVE, dangerous workflow)
– At least 8 sufficient trust signals
– Admin review with documented notes

Certification is monitored daily. If your score drops below thresholds, certification is automatically revoked.
